Grid moving

Visual scale can reduce or enlarge the circle display. The grid moves with the parchment when you use two fingers.

Reading and measurement

  • Read announces the circle's estimated diameter in centimetres or metres.
  • The counter can appear beside the circle or be hidden from the lower settings.
  • One grid cell represents 5 cm; below 5 cm or above 5 m, the value turns red and activation is refused.

Object support

The circle always stays on paper. No support is active by default. For now, the flying shoe is the only available object support.

Activation

  • By default, the spell starts only when Activate is pressed.
  • The automatic option starts the spell when a closed seal is detected.
  • An open or incomplete ring must not launch the scene.

3D scene

Desk Used for normally sized seals: table, books, potions, lamp, and tools.
Outside Used when the circle needs more room: courtyard, workshop, path, mountains, and vegetation.

The 3D scene always places the circle on paper. When shoes are selected, they appear above the parchment rather than making the circle itself fly.

Physical scale

The magic circle shows its true estimated value. Circles below 5 cm or above 5 m remain visible as size errors but cannot be activated.

Export

Archive PNG saves the parchment exactly as drawn at the active scale.

Recognition

Accuracy measures how closely the central sigil resembles the recognized symbol. Signs around the circle, including freehand signs, are read as effects and do not replace the material sigil.

Navigation

Escape closes the 3D view or open panel. Command/Ctrl + Z undoes, and Command/Ctrl + Shift + Z redoes.
